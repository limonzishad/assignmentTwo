Fuji Film X-T10

The Fujifilm X-T10 is a smaller and lighter version of the X-T1. It too features a 16MP X-Trans CMOS sensor and support for X-mount lenses and adapters. The body is magnesium alloy and features dials for shutter speed, aperture, and exposure mode on its top plate. Other features include a tilting 3" LCD, 2.36M dot electronic viewfinder, a 1/32000 sec max shutter speed, 1080/60p video and, of course, Wi-Fi.
